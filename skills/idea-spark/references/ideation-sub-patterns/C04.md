# C04
_parent: **Decompose for Differentiated Treatment** (`heterogeneous_decomposition`)_

## Tactical pattern

This cluster compresses an over-provisioned trained artifact — model weights, intermediate activations, the KV/state buffer, the sampling trajectory, or a dataset — by exploiting heterogeneity in its components rather than applying one uniform lossy operation. The concrete move: discriminate components by a magnitude-, sensitivity-, influence-, or redundancy-based criterion into a high-impact minority and a near-redundant bulk, then preserve, migrate, residually correct, or retain-at-full-precision the minority while aggressively quantizing, pruning, factorizing, or distilling the bulk. Sibling variants relocate the difficulty instead of stratifying it (offline scale-migration from activations to weights, norm-preserving outlier redistribution across groups, decoupling storage precision from compute precision) or relocate the unit of compression itself (operator-pair joint factorization, nested subsets, per-stage variance-guided formats, two-teacher-steps-to-one trajectory halving). The load-bearing artifact is an error/sensitivity decomposition that bounds how the differentiated treatment recovers the original objective — that analysis, not the partition idea, is what separates the move from heuristic per-component tuning.

## Step-by-Step

1. Empirically characterize the resource's component distribution along a candidate discriminating property — magnitude, error-sensitivity, influence, or redundancy — and demonstrate that components fall into structurally distinct populations rather than a homogeneous mass. Do not assert the heterogeneity as folklore or import it from prior work; the value/error distribution that drives the partition is itself a load-bearing piece of evidence, and skipping it leaves reviewers unable to judge either novelty or downstream impact.
2. Define the partition with a criterion tied to the downstream objective that you can defend as distinct from the nearest established discriminator. A proxy any reviewer can immediately recognize as a known technique — roundtrip distortion, raw magnitude, activation covariance, naive double-rounding — will be read as rediscovery unless you show precisely what it captures that the prior criterion does not, and why that difference changes the result.
3. Assign each partition a treatment matched to its property: protect, migrate, residually correct, or retain at full fidelity the high-impact minority, and apply the aggressive lossy operation only to the redundant bulk. When straightforward stratification still leaves a single component dominating the cost, consider relocating the difficulty onto a benign carrier or shifting the unit of compression to a composite that exposes a shared internal degree of freedom invisible at the component level.
4. Supply an error or fidelity decomposition that bounds how the differentiated treatment recovers the uncompressed objective, and make this analysis the spine of the contribution. Without it the method collapses into heuristic per-component tuning that cannot argue the differentiated treatment beats a uniform one — and that uniform-vs-differentiated comparison is the first thing reviewers demand before crediting the partition at all.
5. Validate in the regime where the bottleneck actually bites and against both the strongest uniform baseline and the obvious cheap alternative, reporting the true deployment metric — latency, memory, throughput — rather than a proxy. Toy-scale evidence where the compression problem does not even arise, or proxy-only gains with no measured real-world speedup, will sink an otherwise clean method by leaving its core value proposition unverified.

## When to pick this one

Pick this instantiation when the resource is a trained, over-provisioned artifact whose components carry wildly unequal functional weight — a tiny outlier or high-influence fraction dominates output quality while the bulk is near-redundant — so any uniform compression collapses at the target ratio and the leverage lies in protecting the few while crushing the many.

## Tactical failure mode

The recurring failure is treating the partition criterion itself as the contribution while leaving its value unproven. Papers propose a sensitivity or redundancy discriminator — roundtrip distortion, weight magnitude, activation overlap, double-rounding, subspace projection — and reviewers immediately map it onto an established technique (PCA/SVD on activations, a layer-norm-equivalent rescaling, structured dropout, classical feature distillation), so the differentiated treatment reads as a relabeling rather than a new mechanism. Compounding this, the scheme is rarely benchmarked against both the strongest uniform baseline and the obvious cheap alternative, so no one can confirm the partition actually buys anything over what already exists. A second structural shortcut is validating in a regime where the compression bottleneck does not bite — toy-scale models, proxy-only metrics, no measured inference latency or memory gain — which hollows out the motivation regardless of how clean the method looks. The self-check: can you show why the heterogeneity exists (the value/error distribution that forces it), that the tailored treatment strictly beats uniform treatment plus the nearest prior art, and that the win survives on a real deployment metric in the regime that actually matters?

## Examples
### Oral lessons
- Preserving the small high-influence fraction at full fidelity while crushing the redundant bulk is what keeps extreme compression near-lossless — uniform treatment fails precisely because that sensitive minority absorbs the largest error.
- Outlier difficulty can be a static, relocatable quantity: a norm-preserving rearrangement or an offline scale-migration moves the hard-to-compress mass off the dynamic signal onto a benign carrier, so per-group resolution is no longer hostage to a single extreme value.
- Decoupling storage precision from compute precision — holding parameters compressed but reconstructing to full precision only at the arithmetic boundary — turns 'compression destroys the gradient signal' from a law into an implementation choice.
- Compression error is structured, not noise: factorizing the residual to seed a correction module aligns the compressed start state with the original and determines which optimization basin later adaptation converges to.
- Shifting the unit of compression to a composite — operator pairs, nested subsets, per-stage formats — exposes a shared internal degree of freedom invisible at the single-component level and removes the need for dimension-reconciliation patches.
- A trained system's intermediate representations are a component-agnostic supervision target, so a structurally different replacement can be regressed to locally mimic them and sidestep the cold-start cost of training from scratch.

### Reject lessons
- Presenting a sensitivity or redundancy proxy as the novelty invites the verdict that it rediscovers known mixed-precision or low-rank results unless it is sharply differentiated from the obvious prior criterion.
- Omitting comparison against both the strongest uniform baseline and the obvious cheap alternative (SVD/PCA, structured dropout, a natively trained smaller model) leaves reviewers unable to tell whether the partition buys anything at all.
- Validating a storage or throughput claim only on toy-scale models — where the compression bottleneck does not actually bite — undermines the entire motivation no matter how clean the method is.
- A clever partition with no measured real-world deployment metric (no inference latency or memory speedup) reads as a proxy-only contribution and stalls at borderline.
- Skipping any analysis of why the heterogeneity exists — the data or value distribution that drives the partition — makes the technical novelty and the potential impact impossible to assess.
- Bolting together established components (distillation plus pruning plus calibration) without an error bound or artifact specific to the new setting reads as incremental even when the target application is timely and important.
